Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Peekr (“we”, “our”, “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Peekr.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address
  • Username and display name
  • Profile picture (avatar)
  • Ratings, watchlists, followers, comments, and messages
  • Device and app usage information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide social features and recommendations
  • To improve the app experience
  • To ensure safety and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage & Third Parties

Peekr uses trusted third-party services including Supabase (authentication, database, storage) and The Movie Database (TMDB) for movie and TV metadata.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Sharing of Information

Profile information may be visible to other users depending on your privacy settings. Private messages are only visible to participants.

5. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time. When deleted, personal data is removed or anonymized.

6. Children’s Privacy

Peekr is not intended for children under 13 years old.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
